Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Zelda Guide collects and how it is used, including third-party advertising and cookies. Last updated: July 2026.

1. Information We Collect

Zelda Guide is a free, fan-made guide site. We do not require registration and do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or phone numbers. In some cases, we or our partners may process limited technical data:

  • Access logs: our host may record anonymized IP addresses, browser type, visit times, and page paths for security and analytics.
  • Third-party advertising: when you view pages with ads, ad partners (such as Google AdSense) may use cookies to collect device info, approximate location, and interests to serve relevant ads.
  • Analytics: we may use anonymized analytics to learn which guides are most useful and improve our content.

2. Cookies & Third-Party Ads

This site may display ads served by third parties, including Google. These ad networks may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your visits and serve personalized or relevant ads;
  • Measure ad performance.

You can manage your ad preferences here:

  • Google Ads Settings: google.com/settings/ads
  • Disable or clear cookies in your browser (this may affect ads and some features).

3. How We Use Information

Any information described above is used only to keep the site running, improve our guides, and serve content-relevant ads that help sustain the site. We do not sell your personal information.

4. External Links

Our guides may reference or link to compliant public sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites; please review their own policies.

5. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for a general audience and does not target children under 13. If you are a guardian who believes a child has provided data, contact us and we will address it.

6. Copyright & Site Nature

Zelda Guide is an unofficial fan guide site and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Nintendo. Our guides are original compilations; illustrations use original SVG and AI-generated artwork and do not reuse official assets. Game names, characters, and elements are trademarks of Nintendo.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time; material changes will be announced on the site. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, reach out via the site's repository Issues or public contact channels.
